“Generosity is giving more than you can, and pride is taking less than you need.”

GACAL focuses on that are community-based and are run by local people. As a general rule of , we support grassroot initiatives that are unlikely to recieve supports from international donors. Our six projects provide a variety of services all of which are geared to protect vulunerable children especially orphans and girls.

So far,We have been able to provide:

$ 150.00 to purchase a sewing machine for the economic development of women in rural villages
$ 200.00 monthly salary for a social worker
$ 240.00 annually to sponsor a child/college student
$ 1000.00 to purchase clothing for 100 students
$ 1000.00 annually for a student studying nursing over a four year program
$ 1000.00 annually for a student studying engineering over a four year program
$ 3000.00 salary for a nurse for one year
$ 5000 or $ 10,000 or in any amount for FCN scholarship endowment Fund
