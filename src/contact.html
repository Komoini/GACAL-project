Contact Get A Child A Life Foundation of America

Headquartered in Groveland, CA, the GACAL Foundation of America is a registered and approved 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization providing support to trauma-affected children who have been victims of abuse, neglect or abandonment. Donations to the Children’s Foundation of America are tax deductible in accordance with IRS regulations (EIN: 33-0910478).

If you suspect that a child is in a dangerous situation, contact your county department of human services or police if you believe there is immediate and serious danger to a child or family. You may also call the National Child Abuse Hotline at 1-800-4-A-CHILD.
